What CRT Can Pay For
CRT helps students and RSOs cover academic or professional travel expenses:
Lodging: Hotels near the event
Registration: Conference or competition fees
Travel: Main transportation (flight, bus, etc.)

Yearly Maximums:
RSOs: $4,500 per fiscal year
Individuals: $1,000 per fiscal year
(Fiscal year: July 1 – June 30)

What CRT Won’t Pay For
Mission or cultural exchange trips
Food, social events, or Airbnb stays
Personal/rental vehicles, Uber, or taxis
Study abroad or internships
Trips longer than 30 days or within 50 miles of UCF (unless approved)
Anything bought before meeting with the Business Office

International Travel
Must be approved by UCF Global
Destination must have a Level 3 or lower travel advisory
International students apply 5 business days earlier and complete extra forms

Deadlines
Allocations: Submit ≥ 4 weeks before trip
Bills: Submit ≥ 12 weeks before trip

Bonus Funding (Individuals Only)
+$100 if graduate student
+$250 for Canada, Mexico, Hawaii, or Alaska
+$500 for outside North America
